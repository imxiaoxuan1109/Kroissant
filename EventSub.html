What is the Mystery Box?

A small surprise box that includes one random pastry.
The pastry comes from the day’s leftover items — still fresh, just not sold.

Why we created it

This box is made for customers who:
don’t know what to eat
want something cheap + fun
enjoy trying different pastries.
— and it helps the café reduce leftovers at night —
turning extra pastries into a cute surprise instead
of wasting them.
